About Dev Sthan Bajrang Bali Seva Samiti

We are a registered charitable trust working with underserved communities to create opportunities rooted in dignity, equity, and hope.

Our story

Started with one learning center. Growing through community trust.

Dev Sthan Bajrang Bali Seva Samiti began in 2012 when a small group of teachers, doctors, and social workers came together to support children who were at risk of leaving school. Today, our programs reach thousands through partnerships with volunteers, donors, local institutions, and community leaders.

Our team believes development must be participatory. We listen first, design with local stakeholders, and measure progress with clear indicators.

Our values

The principles behind every project.

Compassion

We serve with empathy and respect for every person's dignity.

Transparency

We report how resources are used and what outcomes are achieved.

Inclusion

We design programs that welcome all genders, abilities, and backgrounds.

Sustainability

We build local capacity so positive change continues long term.
